Sea Otter (Enhydra Iutris)

Otters are keen to use tools such as rocks to open up shellfish, one of their favorite food!

  • Habitat: North America
  • Weight: 31 to 99 lb
  • Length: 3' 3" to 4' 11"
  • Lifespan: 10 to 20 years

Sea otters have the denset fur of any animal with nearly 1 million hairs per square inch. Female sea otters are known to adopt pups and raise them as their own. A group of sea otters is known as a romp and they enjoy playing together, espeically making slides on the shore!
